Scrambled Egg Muffins

Description

These savory muffins are made with sausage, eggs, cheese, and veggies. They're perfect for meal prepping and an easy breakfast option for busy mornings.

Ingredients

  • ½ lb pork sausage
  • 12 eggs
  • ½ cup chopped onion
  • ½ cup chopped green bell pepper
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp pepper
  • ¼ tsp garlic powder
  • ½ cup shredded Cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a muffin tin.
  2. Cook sausage over medium heat until browned. Drain.
  3. In a bowl, whisk eggs and add all other ingredients including sausage.
  4. Pour into muffin cups and bake for 20–25 minutes until set.
  5. Let cool slightly before serving.
