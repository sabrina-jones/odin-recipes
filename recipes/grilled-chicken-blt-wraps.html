Grilled Chicken BLT Wraps

Description

These wraps combine grilled chicken, crispy bacon, lettuce, and tomato for a quick, satisfying lunch. Wrap it all up in a tortilla and you're good to go.

Ingredients

  • 2 grilled chicken breasts, sliced
  • 4 slices cooked bacon
  • 2 cups chopped lettuce
  • 1 tomato, diced
  • 2 large flour tortillas
  • Optional: Ranch or mayo

Steps

  1. Warm tortillas in the microwave or on a skillet.
  2. Layer chicken, bacon, lettuce, and tomato on each tortilla.
  3. Add a spoon of ranch or mayo if desired.
  4. Roll the wraps tightly, slice in half, and serve.
