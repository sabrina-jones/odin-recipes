Quick Beef Stir Fry

Description

This stir fry is packed with beef and fresh vegetables in a quick soy-garlic sauce. It’s simple, flavorful, and great for busy weeknights.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb beef sirloin, sliced thin
  • 2 cups frozen or fresh stir-fry vegetables
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced

Steps

  1. Toss beef with soy sauce and cornstarch. Let sit 10 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in pan and cook garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add beef and cook until browned.
  4. Add vegetables and stir-fry 5–7 minutes until tender.
  5. Serve over rice or noodles if desired.
